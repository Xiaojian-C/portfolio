# Chinese International Students as Tourism Actors in Catalonia

## Short Description

This research poster visualizes the tourism potential of Chinese international students in Catalonia. The project translates survey findings and academic research into a structured information design, highlighting student mobility, recommendation behavior, Chinese digital platforms, and strategic opportunities for destination management.

## Role

Research synthesis, information design, data visualization, layout design, strategic communication.

## Tools

Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop.

## Case Study Structure

1. Hero crop
2. Research question
3. Methodology
4. Key findings
5. Conceptual model
6. Strategic opportunities
7. Full research board
8. Reflection

## Key Message

The project shows how research can be transformed into a clear visual argument, connecting data, theory, and practical tourism strategy.

# COFFEVEL - Coffee & Travel Agency Brand Identity

## Short Description

COFFEVEL is a brand identity concept for a coffee and travel agency. The project combines the everyday ritual of coffee with the emotional idea of movement, routes, and discovery. The visual system uses a minimal coffee-cup symbol, an airplane path, bold typography, and monochrome applications across posters, business cards, packaging, and merchandise.

## Role

Graphic design, logo design, brand applications, poster design, mockup presentation.

## Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign.

## Case Study Structure

1. Hero image
2. Brand concept
3. Logo system
4. Typography and color
5. Poster and campaign application
6. Business cards
7. Packaging and merchandise
8. Final overview

## Key Message

The project shows how a simple visual metaphor can grow into a consistent brand system across both print and environmental applications.
